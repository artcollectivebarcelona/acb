Our Story

Art Collective Barcelona was born from the belief that art shouldn't feel exclusive. After years of studying and teaching, we wanted to create a welcoming space where people can learn classical techniques, explore creativity, and connect with others through painting.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced artist, every session is designed to be immersive, inspiring, and fun.

About the Founder: Tanveer Oberai

Tanveer Oberai is a classically trained artist from Mumbai, based in Barcelona. After training in contemporary art at Srishti Institute (India) and completing a 3-year program at the Barcelona Academy of Art, Tanveer mastered oil painting, drawing, and classical atelier techniques.

His work blends realism with modern storytelling, exploring themes of light, form, and liberation from thought. As the founder of Art Collective Barcelona, Tanveer brings his passion for teaching to every workshop ‚Äî guiding students individually, no matter their level, and making art accessible to everyone.
